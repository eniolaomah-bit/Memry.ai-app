# Memry.ai - Your Second Brain, Fully Remembered

> Never forget what you see, read, or hear—even when you’re too busy to remember.

## What is Memry.ai?

In today's world, we're bombarded with information. You read an insightful article, have a key point in a meeting, see a memorable quote in a video, and then... life happens. By the end of the day, those valuable moments are lost in the mental clutter.

**Memry.ai is your personal memory assistant.** It automatically and securely captures your digital experiences such as articles, screenshots, notes, and conversations and turns them into a searchable, organized personal memory bank. Think of it as a seamless layer between your mind and your digital life, giving you perfect recall.

## Who Is It For?

Memry.ai is built for anyone who learns, consumes, and creates:

*   **Knowledge Workers:** Product Managers, Executives, Researchers, and Consultants who juggle countless information sources daily.
*   **Students and Academics:** Those who need to remember key points from lectures, research papers, and online resources.
*   **Remote Professionals:** Individuals managing digital multitasking and cognitive overload across multiple tools and meetings.

## Why It Matters

Our brains aren't built for infinite information. Existing tools like Notion or Evernote require you to *remember to document*. Memry.ai flips this model. It works quietly in the background, with your consent, to capture context so you can focus on what truly matters: thinking and creating.

**Key Benefits:**
*   **End Cognitive Overload:** Stop trying to remember everything. Let Memry.ai do the remembering for you.
*   **Unify Your Digital Footprint:** Bring together the scattered pieces of your day - screenshots, articles, notes into one coherent, searchable place.
*   **Find Anything Instantly:** Ask "What was that stats tool mentioned in the podcast last week?" and get an immediate answer.

## Core Features

| Feature | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Auto-Capture Layer** | Seamlessly logs information from your devices (text, screenshots, articles) with explicit user consent. |
| **Privacy Vault** | Your data is encrypted on your device. Your memories are for your eyes only. |
| **Contextual Tagging** | Automatically organizes your memories by topic, source, and time using AI. |
| **Semantic Recall** | Search your memories using natural language, just like you would ask a friend. |
| **Weekly Digest** | Get a gentle AI-generated summary of your week's key insights and moments. |
